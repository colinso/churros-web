Our Business

The origin of churros is unclear. One theory suggests they were brought to Europe from China by the Portuguese. The Portuguese sailed for the Orient and, as they returned from Ming Dynasty China to Portugal, they brought along with them new culinary techniques, including altering dough for youtiao, also known as Youzagwei in southern China, for Portugal. The new pastry soon crossed the border into Spain, where it was modified to have the dough extruded through a star-shaped die rather than pulled (Thanks Wikipedia).

The origin of this churro-liscous business, however, is very clear. Brandon and Courtney Beck were engaged to be married on top of the Pyramid of the Sun in Teotihuacan, Mexico in May of 2017. While they did many things to pass the time until they were to be wed a few months later, one happy surprise that they came across was the utter deliciousness of churro-centric desserts. Weekly, they practiced and perfected these dessert with different ingredients and toppings until they had devised the perfect blend. Wanting to share their delight with everyone, they decided to start Pyramids Churros, which they named after the place where their adventure together had begun.
